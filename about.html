our mission

Our mission is to partner with individuals and organizations to unlock their greatest potential by providing tailored, strategic coaching rooted in trust, excellence, and genuine care. We are committed to helping clients navigate challenges, elevate their leadership, and implement meaningful change so they can thrive at every stage of their personal and professional Odyssey.

our vision

To guide leaders and individuals through the Odyssey of life with clarity, confidence, and purpose, empowering them to achieve exceptional success through personalized, high-quality coaching that honors their unique journey.

Core Values

  • Customer First – We prioritize our clients’ goals, needs, and well-being above all else—providing individualized support that honors their unique journey and ensures they feel guided, heard, and valued every step of the way.
  • Excellence – We deliver high-quality, professional coaching grounded in expertise, accountability, and a commitment to transformative results.
  • Integrity – We build trust through honesty, confidentiality, and doing what is right, creating a safe, reliable partnership throughout the Odyssey of personal and professional growth.
  • Personalization – Every coaching engagement is thoughtfully tailored to the individual or organization, ensuring that strategies, tools, and support are precisely aligned with their unique goals and circumstances. Through customized assessments and data-driven insights, each session delivers a distinctly personalized and highly effective coaching experience.

Meet Your Coach

Richard Pagano, MDiv, MBA

Empowering Your Journey: Transformative Coaching & Consulting with Odyssey CDI

Rich Pagano is a dedicated consultant, executive coach, and organizational development practitioner known for his ability to guide individuals, teams, and communities through meaningful personal and professional transformation. Through his company, Odyssey CDI, Rich works with leaders, organizations, and faith-based communities to strengthen purpose, elevate leadership capacity, and navigate the changing waters of organizational and personal growth.

With a career rooted in working closely with people from every background and experience supporting their emotional, spiritual, and strategic development, Rich brings a unique blend of presence, clarity, and insight to every engagement. His coaching style integrates deep active listening, intentional inquiry, and a highly personalized approach that meets each client exactly where they are. Whether working with executives, emerging leaders, individuals or entire communities, Rich helps clients identify their true direction, uncover their potential, and move toward outcomes that feel both energizing and aligned.

Rich’s work centers on integrity, human potential, and the belief that transformation happens when people understand who they are, what they’re called to, and how to navigate the complexities they face. Through assessments, strategic consultation, and tailored developmental roadmaps, he supports clients in clarifying their mission and identity, strengthening a foundational culture, and creating environments where people and organizations can thrive.

Rich is committed to cultivating spaces of growth, renewal, and purpose, helping clients lead with greater confidence, deeper awareness, and sustainable impact.
